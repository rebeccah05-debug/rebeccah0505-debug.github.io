## Controls

Before you begin playing as your sim, letâ€™s overview the controls for Build Mode and Live Mode.
Camera controls in these modes are identical and are for trackpad and mouse unless otherwise noted.

#### Camera Controls 
>* Moving the camera: Hold left click and drag, or use the WASD or arrow keys.
>* Readjusting camera angle: Hold right click and drag.
>* Zooming in/out: Use the scroll wheel on the mouse. For the trackpad, use two fingers in the middle and scroll up or down.
>* Tilting the camera: Hold down the  scroll wheel on the mouse and move the whole mouse up and down. For the trackpad, hold down the command key and click the arrow keys.
#### In-Game Controls
>* Controlling time: Use the panel in the bottom middle of the screen to speed up, slow down, or pause time.
>* Moving between floors of a building: Click the up and down arrows in the top right.
>* Switching wall views (i.e. full, cutaway, no walls): Click the room icon in the top right.
